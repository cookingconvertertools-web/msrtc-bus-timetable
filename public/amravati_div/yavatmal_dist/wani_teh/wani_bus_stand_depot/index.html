What is the earliest bus from Wani Bus Stand?
The earliest MSRTC buses from Wani Bus Stand usually start around 5:00 AM. Early morning services are available for routes like Yavatmal, Rajura and nearby rural areas.
What is the last bus timing from Wani Bus Stand?
The last buses from Wani generally depart between 8:45 PM and 9:00 PM, depending on the route. Night services are commonly available for Yavatmal, Nagpur and Chandrapur.
Are there direct buses from Wani to Nagpur?
Yes, Wani Bus Stand has direct MSRTC buses to Nagpur. These usually run multiple times a day, including morning, afternoon and evening slots.
How frequently are buses available from Wani to Yavatmal?
