FAQs

What is the earliest bus from Wani Bus Stand?
The earliest MSRTC buses from Wani Bus Stand usually start around 5:00 AM. Early morning services are available for routes like Yavatmal, Rajura and nearby rural areas.
What is the last bus timing from Wani Bus Stand?
The last buses from Wani generally depart between 8:45 PM and 9:00 PM, depending on the route. Night services are commonly available for Yavatmal, Nagpur and Chandrapur.
Are there direct buses from Wani to Nagpur?
Yes, Wani Bus Stand has direct MSRTC buses to Nagpur. These usually run multiple times a day, including morning, afternoon and evening slots.
How frequently are buses available from Wani to Yavatmal?
Buses from Wani to Yavatmal are very frequent. There are multiple services starting early morning till late evening, making it one of the busiest routes from this depot.
Are there long-distance buses from Wani to Nanded or Amravati?
Yes, long-distance MSRTC buses to Nanded and Amravati are available from Wani, usually as scheduled services during daytime hours.
Does Wani Bus Stand have night bus services?
Yes, night bus services are available from Wani for selected routes such as Yavatmal, Nagpur and Chandrapur, depending on the day and demand.
Are buses available from Wani to nearby villages?
Wani Bus Stand operates regular buses to nearby villages like Shivni, Sukli, Vadki, Zari (Jamni), Korpana and Gondpipari. Some village routes may have limited daily trips.
Is Wani Bus Stand suitable for daily office or college commuters?
Yes, many early morning and evening buses are timed for daily commuters traveling to Yavatmal, Chandrapur and nearby towns.
Do bus timings from Wani change during festivals or peak seasons?
During festivals, exams and peak travel seasons, MSRTC may add extra buses or adjust timings based on passenger demand.
Where can I check the latest Wani Bus Stand timetable?
You can check updated Wani Bus Stand timings on MSRTC-related websites, local depot notice boards, or timetable-based informational platforms.
